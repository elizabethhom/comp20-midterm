Martha Leugers-Encherman

In her works, Martha Leugers-Encherman shows her appreciation of Northern Michigan's stunning freshwater lakes and massive blue skies. Through paints and pastels, she portrays the untamed beauty still seen in lakes and beaches as well as the manmade features of the area, from the endless fields and picturesque barns of rural farms to sailboats bobbing in the harbor. Her style is characterized by an Impressionist approach with particularly nuanced color in the water and sky.
