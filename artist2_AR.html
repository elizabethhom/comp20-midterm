Alexis Rockman

Alexis Rockman has depicted a darkly surreal vision of the collision between
civilization and nature -- often apocalyptic scenarios on a monumental scale
for over three decades. In his project The Great Lakes Cycles, he celebrates the
natural majesty and global importance of the Great Lakes while exploring how they
are threatened by factors including climate change, globalization, invasive
species, mass agriculture, and urban sprawl.
